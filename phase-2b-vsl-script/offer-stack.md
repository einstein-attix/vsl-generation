# Offer Stack — Value Architecture
## Phase 2b VSL Script | The Signal at $49/Year

---

## Offer Philosophy

The offer stack serves one purpose: make $49/year feel absurdly cheap relative to the value received. We achieve this through value anchoring (assign dollar values to each component), comparison anchoring (vs. financial advisor fees), and risk reversal (60-day unconditional guarantee).

The viewer should feel the price reveal as *relief*, not sticker shock. By the time we say "$49," they should be bracing for $200–$500.

## The Ascension Path (Mentioned, Not Sold)

The VSL sells The Signal at $49. The premium tiers are mentioned as *context*, not as an upsell pitch. This is critical — aggressive upselling in the VSL erodes trust and triggers the "Stansberry complaint" response.

### How Premium Tiers Are Referenced

> "The Signal at $49 gives you everything I just described — the daily briefings, the portfolio visibility, the alerts, the deep dives. That's the complete experience.
>
> If you decide you want more — real-time options flow alerts, exact position sizing, priority access to the AI's highest-conviction trades — those premium tiers exist. Some subscribers upgrade. Most don't need to. The $49 product is not a teaser. It's the real thing."

### Post-Purchase Ascension (OTOs — Not in VSL)

| Tier | Price | What It Adds | When Offered |
|------|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **OTO 1:** Real-Time Options Alerts | $497/year | Live options flow data, AI-generated options strategies, real-time SMS | Immediately after $49 purchase (order confirmation page) |
| **OTO 2:** Wealth Series Access | $2,997/year | Full access to managed portfolio data ($25K–$100K tier visibility), advanced analytics, quarterly strategy calls | 7 days post-purchase (email offer to engaged subscribers) |

**OTO positioning:**
- OTO 1 is positioned as "speed upgrade" — same data, faster delivery
- OTO 2 is positioned as "institutional access" — see what the $100K clients see
- Neither is pitched in the VSL. The VSL closes the $49 sale. Period.

## Offer Section Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Price clearly stated: $49/year
- [ ] Auto-renewal disclosed: "Annual subscription, auto-renews at current rate"
- [ ] Guarantee terms specific: "Full refund within 60 days, no questions asked"
- [ ] Cancellation process disclosed: "Cancel by email anytime"
- [ ] No fake scarcity: no "only 500 spots" or resetting countdown timers
- [ ] Value anchors are reasonable (based on real competitor pricing)
- [ ] Premium tiers mentioned as context, not pushed as upsell
- [ ] "Not investment advice" language present in offer section
- [ ] Renewal price stated if different from introductory price
- [ ] No income claims or return promises in offer section
