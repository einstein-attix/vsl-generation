# VSL Revision Proposal — Aligning with High-Ticket Research Findings

## Document Purpose
This proposal identifies every change needed to align the current VSL slides (`slides.html`) and voiceover script (`script.md`) with the findings in `high-ticket-vsl-cta-research.md`. No changes are to be made until this proposal is approved.

---

## Executive Summary

The current VSL was built as a **signal publication sales tool** with visible pricing and a "Schedule a Call" CTA. The research conclusively shows this approach is wrong for the $25K–$100K price point. The VSL needs to shift from **"here's the price, schedule a call"** to **"this is extraordinary — apply to see if you qualify."**

### The 5 Critical Changes

| # | Issue | Current | Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Pricing shown in VSL** | $8,333/$16,667/$33,333 visible across 10+ slides | Remove ALL pricing — let the sales call handle it |
| 2 | **CTA is "Schedule a Call"** | Passive, unqualified, no exclusivity | "Apply Now — See If You Qualify" (application-first) |
| 3 | **Product model mismatch** | "Signal publisher — you decide how to act" | "AI-managed portfolios via RIA partner" |
| 4 | **Mass-market positioning** | "$23/day, less than lunch"; compared to Motley Fool | "Private research firm"; anchor against Bloomberg ($32K/yr) |
| 5 | **FOMO-based urgency** | "Watching from the outside" / pulsing "LIVE" badges | Milestone-based scarcity, capacity limits, application exclusivity |

## CHANGE 3: Update Product Model — Signal Publisher → AI-Managed Portfolios via RIA

### The Fundamental Shift

The current VSL positions the product as a **signal publication** — "TradeAlgo publishes signals, you decide how to act." But the actual product at $25K–$100K is **AI-managed portfolios executed by an RIA partner**.

This is the single largest content change required.

### Current Positioning (WRONG for $25K–$100K)
- "TradeAlgo publishes signals. That's all we do." (slides 22, 54)
- "TradeAlgo publishes signals. We never touch your money." (slide 43)
- "You decide how to act." (slides 26, 42, 46, 54)
- "Like Motley Fool or Stansberry Research" (slides 22, 60)
- "Signal publisher" framing throughout
- "Optional: prefer hands-free? We work with a brokerage partner" (slide 43)

### Required Positioning (for $25K–$100K auto-traded portfolios)
- TradeAlgo builds the AI and generates the signals
- A licensed RIA partner manages the portfolios and executes all trades
- The subscriber's capital is managed by the RIA partner, held at an institutional custodian
- TradeAlgo is NOT an RIA, does NOT manage money, does NOT hold funds
- The "hands-free" execution is the DEFAULT, not optional

## CHANGE 5: Replace FOMO Urgency with Real Scarcity

### Research Finding
> Countdown timers, "LIVE" pulsing badges, "watching from the outside" = amateur internet marketing that repels HNW buyers
>
> Real scarcity: cohort-based enrollment, capacity limits (alpha decay), milestone-based urgency, application-based exclusivity

### Current Urgency Tactics to Change

| Slide | Current Urgency | Issue | Proposed Replacement |
|---|---|---|---|
| **63** | "LIVE" pulsing badge + "While you've been watching this video, the AI has been analyzing..." | Feels like a gimmick. The "LIVE" pulsing badge is the exact kind of tactic the research says repels sophisticated buyers. | Keep the factual statement about the AI running. Remove the "LIVE" pulsing animation. Reframe: "The AI runs continuously. It evaluates 86 stocks every week, generates signals, and the RIA partner executes. The system doesn't stop." — factual, not gimmicky. |
| **64** | "Every week you wait is a week of AI-powered intelligence you're not receiving" | FOMO pressure language. Research says: "opportunity cost framing" is better than "you're missing out" framing | Reframe as opportunity cost: "Every signal cycle generates documented decisions across 86 stocks. The system produces roughly 200 signals per year per portfolio — each with full AI reasoning. The question isn't whether the AI works. It's whether you want it working for your portfolio." |
| **65** | "How many more weekly rebalances do you want to watch from the outside?" | Classic FOMO / inside-outside framing | Replace with capacity-based scarcity: "We limit enrollment to maintain signal quality and portfolio performance. When too many investors follow identical signals, alpha decays — the edge erodes. That's why we review every application and cap enrollment at each tier." |
| **69** | "AI STATUS: ACTIVE" with pulsing dot + "The only question is whether you'll be watching from the inside — or the outside" | Pulsing status indicator + FOMO | Remove pulsing "ACTIVE" badge. Replace with factual status: "Last rebalance: March 4. Next cycle: This week. All 14 portfolios updated." Then replace FOMO with: "Submit your application below. If you're a fit, we'll schedule your private strategy session within 48 hours." |

### Script Lines That Need Urgency Revision

| Script Location | Current | Proposed |
|---|---|---|
| Slide 63 VO | "While you've been watching this, the AI hasn't stopped. It's evaluating. It's scoring." | Keep but tone down: "The AI runs continuously. While we've been talking, it's been evaluating positions, scoring stocks, and preparing for the next rebalance cycle." Remove breathless urgency tone direction. |
| Slide 64 VO | "Every week you wait is a week of AI-driven intelligence you're not accessing." | "Every week, the system generates a new cycle of documented decisions across 86 stocks. The track record grows. The AI improves. And founding member pricing won't last indefinitely." |
| Slide 65 VO | "TradeAlgo can only onboard so many new subscribers..." + "how many more weekly signal cycles you want to watch from the outside" | "We accept a limited number of investors per quarter — this is a genuine constraint, not a marketing tactic. The AI's edge depends on signal exclusivity. Submit your application, and we'll review it personally." |
| Slide 69 VO | "The only question is whether you'll be watching from the inside — or the outside." | "The system is running. The signals are being generated. The question is whether you want this AI working for your portfolio — and whether you qualify." |

### New Scarcity Elements to Add

Per the research, these real scarcity mechanisms should be woven into slides 63–69:

1. **"Limited to [X] founding members per tier"** — Alpha decay is real. More investors following identical signals = less alpha. This justifies genuine capacity limits.
2. **"Enrollment opens quarterly"** — Cohort-based onboarding. "Q2 2026 cohort is now accepting applications."
3. **"Founding member pricing"** — "Current pricing reflects founding member rates. As the AI's track record lengthens, pricing increases to reflect proven performance. Current members are locked in permanently."
4. **"Application required"** — The application itself IS the scarcity mechanism. "Not everyone is accepted."
