Research Recommendations — Implementation Status

How each recommendation from the high-ticket research maps to the current VSL.

2.1 Refundable Deposit Model
Not Implemented
Use a $1,000 fully refundable deposit. Apply to full purchase price if they proceed. 14-day decision window.Research — Section 2, lines 179–208

Recommended flow: VSL → Application → Deposit ($1,000) → Strategy Call → Close

Current flow: VSL → "Apply Now — See If You Qualify" → (nothing specified)

What's missing:

  • No mention of deposit anywhere in slides or script
  • No deposit amount stated
  • No "fully refundable" language
  • No 14-day decision window mentioned
  • Post-application flow is vague — slide 68 says "We'll Reach Out" but doesn't specify what happens

Research data supporting this:

  • Perry Belcher: "$500 deposit cuts lead volume 60% but increases close rate 300%"
  • Russ Ruffino: $1K deposits for $30K–$50K coaching → 40–60% close rates
  • Tesla pre-orders: $250–$1,000 deposit → 70–80% purchase rate
  • Endowment effect (Kahneman): People value things they own 2–3x more

What is NOT implemented:

  • No comparison to hedge fund minimums ($500K–$5M+)
  • No comparison to Gavekal/BCA Research ($25K–$250K/yr)
