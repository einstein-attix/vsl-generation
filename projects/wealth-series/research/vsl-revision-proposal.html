Aligning the current VSL slides and voiceover script with high-ticket research findings for the $25K–$100K price point. Every change is documented with before/after comparisons.

Executive Summary

The 5 Critical Changes

The current VSL was built as a signal publication sales tool with visible pricing and a "Schedule a Call" CTA. The research conclusively shows this approach is wrong for $25K–$100K. The VSL must shift from "here's the price, schedule a call" to "this is extraordinary — apply to see if you qualify."

Change 3: Signal Publisher → AI-Managed Portfolios via RIA

This is the single largest content change required. The current VSL positions the product as a signal publication — "you decide how to act." The actual product at $25K–$100K is AI-managed portfolios executed by an RIA partner.

Current Positioning (Wrong)Required Positioning (Right)
"TradeAlgo publishes signals. That's all we do." TradeAlgo builds the AI and generates the signals
"You decide how to act." A licensed RIA partner manages the portfolios and executes all trades
"Like Motley Fool or Stansberry Research" Subscriber capital managed by RIA, held at institutional custodian
"Optional: prefer hands-free?" Hands-free execution IS the default product

Change 5: FOMO → Real Scarcity

Countdown timers, "LIVE" pulsing badges, and "watching from the outside" language = amateur internet marketing that repels HNW buyers. Use real scarcity: cohort-based enrollment, capacity limits (alpha decay), milestone-based urgency, application-based exclusivity. — High-Ticket VSL Research Report
Slide 63 "LIVE" Pulsing Badge
Before

"LIVE" pulsing badge + "While you've been watching this video, the AI has been analyzing 86 stocks..."

Pulsing badges are the #1 tactic research says repels sophisticated buyers.

After

Remove pulsing "LIVE" animation.

Keep factual statement. Reframe: "The AI runs continuously. It evaluates 86 stocks every week, generates signals, and the RIA partner executes. The system doesn't stop."

Slide 64 FOMO Pressure
Before

"Every week you wait is a week of AI-powered intelligence you're not receiving"

After

Opportunity cost framing: "Every signal cycle generates documented decisions across 86 stocks. The system produces roughly 200 signals per year per portfolio. The question isn't whether the AI works. It's whether you want it working for your portfolio."

Slide 65 "Watching from the outside"
Before

"How many more weekly rebalances do you want to watch from the outside?"

After

Capacity-based scarcity: "We limit enrollment to maintain signal quality and portfolio performance. When too many investors follow identical signals, alpha decays — the edge erodes. That's why we review every application and cap enrollment at each tier."

Slide 69 "AI STATUS: ACTIVE" Pulsing Dot
Before

"AI STATUS: ACTIVE" with pulsing dot + "The only question is whether you'll be watching from the inside — or the outside"

After

Remove pulsing "ACTIVE" badge.

Factual status: "Last rebalance: March 4. Next cycle: This week. All 14 portfolios updated."

"Submit your application below. If you're a fit, we'll schedule your private strategy session within 48 hours."

New Scarcity Mechanisms to Add

  1. "Limited to [X] founding members per tier" — Alpha decay is real. More investors following identical signals = less alpha.
  2. "Enrollment opens quarterly" — Cohort-based onboarding. "Q2 2026 cohort is now accepting applications."
  3. "Founding member pricing" — "As the AI's track record lengthens, pricing increases. Current members are locked in permanently."
  4. "Application required" — The application itself IS the scarcity mechanism. "Not everyone is accepted."
