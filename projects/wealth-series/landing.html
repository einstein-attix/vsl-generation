Frequently Asked Questions

Wealth Series portfolios are generated by PilotAI and evaluated against a qualifying scale. The AI continuously monitors holdings and triggers rebalancing events based on threshold changes. Each rebalance generates written rationale at both the portfolio level (macro/sector sentiment) and stock level (key drivers, growth trends). All signals are published with timestamps so you can verify every decision.
The five narratives are: Growth (high-growth sectors and momentum plays), Low-Risk Volatility (reduced drawdown strategies), Deep Value/Fundamentals (undervalued companies with strong financials), Defensive (capital preservation with gold, consumer staples, and short hedges), and Balanced (diversified mix across all narratives). Each portfolio is mapped to one or more of these strategies.
Premium gives you AI-generated signals with written reasoning — you read the signals and execute trades yourself through your own brokerage. Auto includes everything in Premium but also trades the portfolios on your behalf through a brokerage partner, removing the need to manage trades yourself. Same AI, same signals — Auto just adds hands-off execution.
Lite is an entry-level AI stock picker product. You pick stocks from 4 AI-powered models (Fundamentals First, Trend Tracker, Sentiment Sentry, Value and Growth) to build your own custom portfolio of up to 10 stocks. Your portfolio competes on a public leaderboard ranked by P&L. Think of it as a fantasy league for investing — powered by AI stock picks refreshed biweekly.
Weekly analyst sessions are hosted by Dane and Eric. Atlas members join on Mondays (45 min), Titan on Tuesdays (45 min), and Everest on Wednesdays (75 min) — all at 4:30 PM EST. These are live, interactive sessions with Q&A — not pre-recorded webinars. Lite members also get weekly livestream access.
