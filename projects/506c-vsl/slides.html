Section 6 — Risk Disclosure
What You Need to Know
Before You Invest

Risk 1 — Material
SEC Investigation
TA Fintech Inc. is currently under investigation by the Securities and Exchange Commission. This investigation could result in civil monetary penalties, disgorgement of funds, injunctive relief, enforcement actions, and reputational harm. The nature, scope, timeline, and potential outcomes have not been fully detailed. This risk could materially affect the company and your investment.
Source: PPM (Nov 3, 2025). SEC investigation is a material disclosure requirement.
Risk 2
No Guarantee of Returns
The company has never been profitable. Accumulated deficit as of December 31, 2025: $107.9 million. The company expects to continue operating at a loss for an extended period. You could lose your entire investment.
Source: Unaudited Financial Statements (Dec 31, 2025) and PPM (Nov 3, 2025).
Risk 3
Shares Are Illiquid
No public market exists for these shares. None is expected to develop. This is an illiquid investment. You should be prepared to hold indefinitely. Shares cannot be freely sold or transferred — they are restricted securities under the Securities Act.
Source: PPM (Nov 3, 2025).
Risk 4
Cash Position & Burn Rate
Cash at December 31, 2025: $1.8 million. Operating cash burn in FY2025: ~$26.9 million (~$2.2M/month). The company also holds $12.8M in investment securities. Without this offering or additional financing, the company's ability to continue operations is limited. Full raise targets ~36 months of runway.
Source: Unaudited Financial Statements (Dec 31, 2025).
Risk 5
Valuation Premium
The $1.15B pre-money valuation represents an 88.7x multiple on current revenue ($12.9M). Comparable public SaaS companies typically trade at 6–25x revenue. This valuation is based on future growth expectations, not current profitability. No independent appraisal has been conducted.
Source: Calculated from PPM and Financial Statements.
Risk 6
Controlling Shareholder
Carlos Cruz holds ~56% of outstanding shares and proxy voting rights from other investors — effective sole control. New investors will not have meaningful voting power. The controlling stake was acquired for $2,000,000 in June 2025.
Source: PPM (Nov 3, 2025).
Additional Risks
No Registered IP
No patents or trademarks. Protection via trade secrets and NDAs only.
R&D Intensity
R&D is 244% of revenue. Profitability requires dramatic revenue growth or R&D reduction.
Unaudited FY2025
FY2025 financial statements are unaudited. Material adjustments could emerge.
Revenue Recognition
Multi-year contracts create gaps between cash collected and GAAP revenue.
Source: PPM (Nov 3, 2025) and Financial Statements.
