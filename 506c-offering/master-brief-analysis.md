Democratize institutional-grade market intelligence through AI. Proprietary systems process millions of data points from filings, exchanges, and macroeconomic sources in real time.

### Corporate Description

TA Fintech develops proprietary AI-driven software and data-analytics systems that enhance financial transparency and automation for investors. Its technology processes large volumes of real-time market, economic, and behavioral data, converting that information into visual intelligence, alerts, and pattern-recognition outputs that help users interpret market dynamics. ### Revenue Channels

1. **Direct-to-Consumer (D2C):** Freemium-to-paid funnel driving individual subscriber upgrades via platform
2. **Third-Party RIA Collaboration:** Company currently collaborates with a duly licensed RIA firm for certain licensed activities
3. **Institutional Licensing:** Midas Link and model portfolio frameworks licensed to RIA partners, family offices, and institutional clients
4. **Regulated Financial Services (Exploratory):** Pursuing proprietary RIA registration and additional regulated financial service offerings — all subject to regulatory approvals. No such services are currently active under the Company's own license
5. **Newsletter (Proposed):** 'The Signal' — tiered AI newsletter product leveraging Wealth Series analytics data. Not yet launched

### Revenue Recognition

- Wealth Series contracts recognized over **24 months**
- Midas Link contracts recognized over **36 months**
- GAAP revenue ≠ cash received in any given period
- Deferred revenue of $8.7M+ (Aug 2025) / $15.1M (Dec 2025) represents future service obligations already paid for

### PPM Product Positioning Note

The PPM describes Wealth Series as an "analytical & signal generation tool" where "third-party custodians hold assets" and "licensed RIAs make investment decisions." This language carefully positions the product as a signal publication rather than money management. Consumer-facing materials describe the same product with more consumer-oriented language ("AI-powered investment portfolio management solution").

Implied Sep–Dec revenue is negative $(2.4M) — not mathematically possible for a subscription SaaS business unless revenue was restated, reclassified, or reversed. In a $120M offering, a $2.4M gap between PPM and auditable financials is material.

**Investors relying on the PPM's 8-month figure to extrapolate annual run-rate would arrive at ~$23M annualized — nearly double the actual $12.94M FY result.** The marketing brief continues to cite the ~$23M annualized run rate.
