## Executive Summary

TA Fintech, Inc. is a Delaware C corporation (incorporated Sep 24, 2021) operating as a subscription-based software service provider in the fintech/big data space. The company's 2025 unaudited financials reveal:

- **Revenue growth of 34.6%** — net revenue rose from $9.62M (2024) to $12.94M (2025)
- **Continued net losses** — net loss of $(35.5M) in 2025, improved from $(49.2M) in 2024 (28% improvement)
- **Significant cash burn** — cash decreased from $21.79M to $1.79M, a $(20.0M) decline
- **Heavy equity investment portfolio** — $12.83M in equity securities (up from $5.27M)
- **Equity financing dependent** — $15.37M raised through stock issuance in 2025
- **Total assets contracted 43%** — from $28.04M to $16.04M
- **Accumulated deficit of $(107.98M)** — reflecting ongoing pre-profitability stage

## Observations & Risk Factors

### Strengths
1. **Strong revenue growth (34.6%)** — demonstrates product-market traction
2. **Improving gross margins (71.8%)** — software model scaling well
3. **Operating loss narrowing** — from $(45.4M) to $(36.5M), a 20% improvement
4. **Deferred revenue surge (119%)** — $15.07M of prepaid subscriptions suggests strong forward bookings
5. **G&A cost discipline** — 39% reduction year-over-year
6. **Clean legal/compliance profile** — no lawsuits, no tax disputes

### Risks & Concerns
1. **Critical cash position** — only $1.79M cash remaining with $(26.9M) annual operating burn; **immediate financing needed**
2. **Near-total capital consumption** — accumulated deficit of $(108M) against $(109M) total capital raised (99.2% consumed)
3. **Stockholders' equity near zero** — $0.9M equity on $16M in assets; leverage ratio 16.8x
4. **Concentrated investments** — $12.83M in equity securities at a single broker (IBKR) creates market risk and concentration risk
5. **FDIC exposure reduced but still present** — cash exceeded FDIC limits by $1.3M
6. **R&D spend exceeds revenue by 2.4x** — heavy investment phase; path to profitability unclear
7. **Reduced equity raise** — only $15.4M raised in 2025 vs. $63.1M in 2024; fundraising capacity may be tightening
8. **Unaudited financials** — 2025 numbers not yet audited (subsequent events evaluated through Jan 7, 2026)
9. **Single product/market dependency** — subscription software for stock market investing
10. **Going concern risk** — cash runway of approximately 0.8 months at current burn rate without additional financing
