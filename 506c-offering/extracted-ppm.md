# COMPANY OVERVIEW

## Incorporation & History
- Originally incorporated as **Broad Bold Park, Inc.**, Delaware, September 9, 2021
- Renamed **TA Fintech Inc.** — fintech innovation & AI analytics focus
- SaaS model — financial data products, AI analytics, visualization tools

## Mission
Democratize institutional-grade market intelligence through AI. Proprietary systems process millions of data points from filings, exchanges, and macroeconomic sources in real time.

## Products and Services
- **TradeGPT** — AI copilot: real-time data interpretation, sentiment analysis, NLP search
- **Dashboard/Terminal/Premium/Platinum Plans** — Subscription tiers with real-time market data, dark-pool analytics, AI sentiment, advanced dashboards
- **Wealth Series (Atlas|Titan|Everest)** — Analytical & signal generation tool; third-party custodians hold assets; licensed RIAs make investment decisions
- **Options Academy** — Educational content & interactive simulations
- **Midas Link** — Institutional-grade analytics models licensed through RIA partners

## Competition
Bloomberg Terminal, FactSet, Morningstar Direct — legacy, costly, closed. TA Fintech differentiates via AI-first architecture, explainable algorithms, multi-dataset integration, scalable SaaS model.

## Intellectual Property
- No registered patents or trademarks
- Trade secrets, business methods, know-how
- IP assignment and confidentiality agreements with employees/advisors
- Licensed IP through API vendors, cloud services, market data from all 19 exchanges
