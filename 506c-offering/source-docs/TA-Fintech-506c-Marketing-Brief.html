01 Company Identity

TA Fintech Inc.

TA Fintech develops proprietary AI-driven software and data-analytics systems that enhance financial transparency and automation for investors. Its technology processes large volumes of real-time market, economic, and behavioral data, converting that information into visual intelligence, alerts, and pattern-recognition outputs that help users interpret market dynamics.

Operating under a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model, TA Fintech provides financial-data products, AI-based analytics, and visualization tools to investors, financial professionals, and institutional clients. The platform unifies data research, market analytics, and algorithmic modelling within an AI environment. At present, all internal operations are focused on software analytics, data delivery, and research technology. The Company currently collaborates with a duly licensed Registered Investment Adviser (RIA) firm for certain licensed activities, and is actively developing its financial services infrastructure — including pursuing registration of a proprietary RIA and exploring additional regulated financial service offerings — all subject to applicable regulatory approvals and compliance requirements. TA Fintech Inc. is the legal issuer of record for this Reg D Rule 506(c) offering.

Financial Risks

  • Accumulated deficit of $93M+ as of August 2025; ~$2.3M/month operating cash burn.
  • Cash position may not be sufficient if additional capital cannot be raised on acceptable terms.
  • Equity investment portfolio ($13.2M Vanguard/IBKR as of Aug 2025) exposes company to market price risk.
  • Continued operations dependent on new equity raises or achieving operating profitability.
