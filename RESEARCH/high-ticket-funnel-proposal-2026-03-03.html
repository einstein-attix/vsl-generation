This funnel has seven stages, each with a specific conversion mechanism. Let's break down each one.

Stage 1: Free Subscriber Acquisition

At $10K/day and $3-5 per subscriber, we acquire 2,000-3,333 new free subscribers daily (60K-100K/month). Channels: Facebook/Instagram ads targeting self-directed investors, newsletter cross-promotions ($1-2/sub), boost networks (beehiiv, SparkLoop at $2-3/sub), and organic/referral (free).

Stage 2: Engagement Qualification

Not every subscriber is a lead. We filter for engagement: subscribers who open 5+ of their first 10 emails demonstrate the reading habit that precedes purchase intent. Industry benchmark: 60% of financial newsletter subscribers maintain this engagement level in the first 30 days. The other 40% are ad-revenue-only subscribers.

Stage 3: Behavioral Scoring

Engaged subscribers accumulate a lead score based on specific actions (detailed in Section 3). When a subscriber crosses the score threshold of 50 points, they enter the active nurture pipeline. Approximately 25% of engaged subscribers reach this threshold within 60 days.

Stage 4: Automated Nurture Sequences

Scored leads enter product-specific email sequences (detailed in Section 4). The sequences are designed around "aha moments" — the instant a lead viscerally understands the value. For TradeAlgo, the biggest aha moment is seeing the AI's portfolio beat the market during a volatile week.

Stage 5: Webinar / VSL Attendance

Financial services webinars achieve 61% attendance rate — the highest of any industry (ON24 Benchmarks 2025). We target 16% of scored leads attending at least one webinar or watching a VSL to completion. Stansberry's "End of America" VSL — 77 minutes, single take — generated $300M in total revenue. The content quality here is everything.

Stage 6: Appointment Booking

Webinar attendees who show purchase signals (stayed for the CTA, clicked the booking link, visited the pricing page) are routed to a setter who qualifies them and books a strategy session with a closer. Industry benchmark: 20% of engaged webinar attendees book an appointment. In financial services, reaching out within 5 minutes of a high-intent action is 21x more effective than waiting 30 minutes.

Stage 7: Sales Team Closes

The closer conducts a strategy session or portfolio review. These leads have been reading the newsletter for weeks, attended a webinar, and self-selected for a call. Close rates of 20-30% on booked appointments are realistic for this quality of lead (industry average for high-ticket qualified appointments: 15-29%).

5. Appointment Funnel Mechanics

How Warm Leads Become Booked Calls

The goal is simple: get pre-qualified leads in front of the sales team with maximum context and minimum friction. Four pathways lead to a booked appointment:

Pathway 1: Webinar → Strategy Session

The primary appointment engine. Weekly live webinar (or evergreen replay) ends with a direct booking CTA. Webinar attendees who stay past the 40-minute mark are the highest-intent leads. Smaller webinars convert dramatically better — 100-200 attendees convert at 50% vs. 6% for 1,000+ (ON24 data). Recommendation: run niche-targeted webinars ("How AI Manages a $500K Portfolio") rather than broad ones.

Pathway 2: Portfolio Review Offer

Subscribers who visit the portfolio dashboard 3+ times or click on the $100K model sizing content receive a personalized email: "Want a free portfolio review? We'll show you how Cai's AI would allocate your specific portfolio." This is a Trojan horse for the sales conversation — the review naturally surfaces where TradeAlgo's products solve gaps in their current strategy.

Pathway 3: Urgency Triggers

Market events create natural urgency windows. When the VIX spikes above 25 or the S&P drops 2%+ in a day, high-score leads receive: "Markets just dropped X%. Here's what Cai's AI did — and what you should consider for your own portfolio. Book a 15-minute strategy call." These time-sensitive offers convert 3-5x higher than scheduled emails because the need is felt, not manufactured.

Pathway 4: Direct Setter Outreach

When a lead crosses the 100-point score threshold, the setter receives a real-time alert with full engagement history: every email opened, every link clicked, every page visited, webinar attendance, portfolio size (if captured). The setter calls within 5 minutes. Contacting within 5 minutes of a high-intent action is 21x more effective than waiting 30 minutes (Drift/Harvard Business Review). The setter's job: qualify and book a strategy session with a closer within 48 hours.

Critical Detail

The handoff packet. When a setter books an appointment with a closer, the closer receives a one-page lead brief: subscriber since [date], opened [X/Y] emails, attended webinar on [date], visited pricing page [X] times, estimated portfolio size [$X], specific products they clicked on. The closer walks into the call already knowing the lead's journey. This is the difference between a cold pitch and a warm conversation.

SEC Game-Changer

The March 2025 SEC no-action letter dramatically simplified 506(c) verification. Previously, issuers needed third-party verification (CPA letters, tax returns, etc.) costing $250-$500 per investor. Now, a $200K minimum investment + written self-certification satisfies the "reasonable steps" requirement. TradeAlgo's $250K Midas Link tier naturally meets this threshold, potentially allowing seamless integration between the Wealth Series and the 506(c) offering.

11. Implementation Roadmap: 90 Days

Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

  1. CRM setup (HubSpot). Configure deal pipeline stages matching the funnel: Free → Engaged → MQL → SQL → Appointment → Closed. Import existing customer data. Set up lead scoring rules. Owner: Marketing Ops.
  2. Newsletter infrastructure. Migrate or configure newsletter platform (beehiiv). Set up automated welcome sequence (5 emails, Days 0-14). Configure engagement tracking (opens, clicks, page visits). Owner: Content + Engineering.
  3. Tracking & attribution. Implement UTM framework across all newsletter links. Set up Mixpanel/Amplitude event tracking on website (pricing pages, product pages, portfolio dashboard). Connect newsletter engagement data to HubSpot via API or CDP. Owner: Engineering.
  4. Build the first webinar. Script a 45-minute "How Cai's AI Manages $10M+" webinar. Record an evergreen version. Set up registration page + post-webinar email sequence. Owner: Content + Leadership.
  5. Sales team alignment. Brief the setter/closer team on the newsletter funnel. Define the handoff packet format. Set SLAs: setters respond within 5 minutes for 100+ score leads, within 24 hours for 75+ score leads. Owner: Sales Leadership.

Phase 2: Launch (Days 30-60)

  1. Turn on paid acquisition. Start at $5K/day (not $10K) to test and optimize. Target: $3-5 per free subscriber via Meta ads, newsletter cross-promotions, and SparkLoop boost network. Monitor Day 7 and Day 14 engagement rates to assess lead quality by channel. Owner: Growth.
  2. Launch the Options Dashboard self-serve funnel. Day 14 nurture email + landing page + 14-day trial with card required. This is the fastest revenue win — expect first sales within 3 weeks of launch. Owner: Product + Marketing.
  3. Run the first live webinar. Target 200-300 attendees (smaller converts better). Record it. Deploy the evergreen version the following week. Measure: attendance rate, watch time, booking rate. Owner: Content + Sales.
  4. Activate the lead scoring system. Begin scoring all subscribers. After 30 days, review the distribution: what % are reaching 50+, 75+, 100+? Calibrate thresholds based on actual data. Owner: Marketing Ops.
  5. Launch the referral program. 3 referrals = 1 month Premium free. Build a viral loop that reduces blended CAC over time. Morning Brew drove 30% of all growth via referrals at peak. Owner: Growth.
