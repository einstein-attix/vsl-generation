2. The Model: Stansberry's Playbook, Modernized

Stansberry built a $409M business using a tiered subscription model pioneered by Agora Inc. We copy the architecture that works and skip the elements that damage trust or don't fit our strengths.

Copy from Stansberry

  • Tiered pricing pyramid — Free → $149 → $499 → $2,500 → Managed Accounts. Each tier unlocks more data about the same AI portfolios.
  • Self-liquidating entry offer — $149/year priced to break even on customer acquisition cost. Profit starts at $499.
  • One-Time Offer after purchase — Immediately offer Pro upgrade at discounted $299 after Premium signup.
  • Welcome email sequence — 7-day onboarding that builds the habit of opening our emails and establishes trust.
  • Performance-triggered upsells — Big winning trade → upsell email to next tier. The AI's performance does the selling.

Do NOT Copy

  • 77-minute Video Sales Letters — We can't make VSLs and don't need to. Replace with live scoreboard + short social clips.
  • Daily promotional email spam — Stansberry's #1 complaint. Max 2–3 emails/week. One brand. Quality over volume.
  • Credit-not-cash refund trick — Damages trust. Real 30-day money-back guarantee. Our AI's performance keeps refund rates low.
  • Fear-based hype marketing — "End of America" scare tactics create skeptical, low-trust subscribers. Lead with data and track records.
  • Editor-dependent products — The AI is our "editor." It doesn't quit, get sick, or have ethics scandals. No key-person risk.

5. How We Acquire Subscribers (No VSLs)

Stansberry spends 77 minutes on a video selling opinions. We let the AI's track record sell itself. Four acquisition channels replace the VSL entirely.

Live Portfolio Scoreboard

Public page on tradealgo.com showing real-time performance across all 5 portfolios. Always updating. People bookmark it, share it, screenshot it. This is our "VSL" — except it runs 24/7 and costs nothing to maintain. The numbers do the talking.

Short Social Clips (30–60 sec)

Screen recordings of the portfolio dashboard after a big winning trade. "The AI bought NVDA at $X three weeks ago. Here's the P&L today." Run as paid ads on Facebook, YouTube, TikTok. No scripting, no production value needed. Just the scoreboard.

Referral Program

Copy Morning Brew's playbook. Subscribers earn rewards for referring friends — stickers, premium reports, free month of Pro. This was Morning Brew's primary growth engine from 0 to 4.4M subscribers. Near-zero acquisition cost.

SEO + Lead Magnets

AI-generated articles targeting stock ticker keywords. Lead magnets like "Top 10 AI Stock Picks This Month" capture emails at scale. MarketBeat built 5.5M subscribers with this exact approach and a team of 18 people.

Why this works better than VSLs: Stansberry needs 77 minutes because they're selling one person's opinion. We have something they don't — a live, provable track record from 5 AI portfolios running real money. A 30-second clip of the dashboard after a winning trade is more persuasive than any 77-minute video. And it works on TikTok and Instagram Reels, where Stansberry's format is a dinosaur.

7. Email Strategy: Quality Over Volume

Stansberry (What NOT to Do)

  • Multiple promotional emails per day from 5 different brands
  • Fear-based, hype-driven copy and fake urgency
  • #1 subscriber complaint = "too many emails"
  • Result: ~15–20% open rates, high churn, BBB complaints
  • Lost 47% of paid subscribers in 2 years

TradeAlgo (Our Approach)

  • 1 free newsletter per week (content delivery)
  • 1 premium newsletter per week (gated content)
  • Max 1 promotional email per week (if any)
  • Upsell embedded in product (locked content blocks)
  • Target: 40–50% open rates like Morning Brew
